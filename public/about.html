About Munamii Cakery

Meet Anna

Hej, I'm Anna, owner and head pastry chef at Munamii Cakery. I first started to bake with my 'abuelita' (grandmother) when I was five years old and the passion never left! Today, I feel honoured to bring joy to our customers through our cakes, just like my abuelita's baking did to me.

Our promise to you

Every single cake and cupcake that we make is special. Not only do we want to deliver a dessert to your table, we want you to have a unique experience - an unforgettable memory. With over eight years of pastry expertise, you can trust us to hit the sweet spot every purchase!
